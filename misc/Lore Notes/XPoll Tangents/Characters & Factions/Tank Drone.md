Warehouse Alias: Carapace
---
A small bomb diffusal robot with a gun attached to the arm and a reinforced hull. These adaptations allow it to perform in combat, but is often also used to search crawl spaces and navigate hazardous areas to defeat traps.

It is capable of raising and carrying a shield large enough to protect a person, acting as mobile cover.
