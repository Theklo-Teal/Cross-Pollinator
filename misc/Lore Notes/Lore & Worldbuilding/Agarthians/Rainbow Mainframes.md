In Agarthian: "Xielarko", sometimes also "Cerbarko" as a pun, like saying "Brainbow" in English.

These are large computers with [[Orgone]] Transceivers that store and execute the consciousnesses of Devio Agarthians and Agartha. They split the Orgone accumulated by these consciousnesses into Ectoplasm generators for power and to relay into the network with other Mainframes.

The dense Orgone activity they produce in the Noosphere is the origin of Ley Lines.
